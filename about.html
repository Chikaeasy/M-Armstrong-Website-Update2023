Learn About Ms. Armstrong

Attorney and Judge Melodee Armstrong is a native Texan. She graduated from Dartmouth College with awards and distinction. After working two years in the Houston City Attorney’s office, she entered and graduated from the University of Houston Law Center with awards and distinction.

She has interned in the chambers of a federal U.S. Magistrate Judge and been trained at top-tier civil litigation defense law firms. Attorney and Judge Armstrong selflessly donates hours of pro bono legal service to represent individuals fighting for justice. She also conducts pro bono legal workshops and forums and provides pro bono legal consultation to non-profits as well as to entities advocating for criminal justice.

Along with numerous service engagements, she created the Share the Vision global eyeglass initiative, initiated pro bono legal defense for juvenile offenders, and created and funded a court essay contest for elementary and middle school students.
