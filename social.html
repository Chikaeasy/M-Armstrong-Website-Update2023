Our Social & Community Engagement Commitment

At the Law Offices of Melodee Armstrong, we believe in giving back to the community. We are committed to social and community engagement through various initiatives and partnerships aimed at making a positive impact.

Community Outreach

Serving with others on the front line to engage and support children, young adults, and others in need and to excel.

3rd Annual Freedom For All Sneaker Ball

Share The Vision

Est. 2011, Judge Melodee founded Share the Vision, which is her annual birthday benefit focused on guests donating new or gently used eye glasses to be distributed globally as a gift of improved sight to those whom we will never see.

Youth Mentorship

We provide mentorship to young individuals, guiding them towards a brighter future and fostering the next generation of leaders.

Judge Melo In Action

Community Workshops

Hosting workshops, including pro bono, on various topics such as financial literacy, legal rights, and personal development to empower our community members.
